how does cyberbulling affect others

  1. Lower school attendance and poor school performance
  2. Increased stress, anxiety, and anger
  3. Feeling scared, alone, guilty or ashamed
  4. Poor concentration

Based on these effects on cyber bulling tells us why we need to prevent it.

When bullying happens online it can feel as if you’re being attacked everywhere, even inside your own home. It can seem like there’s no escape.

The effects can last a long time and affact a person in many ways:

  • Mentally - feeling upset, embarrassed, stupid, even angry.
  • Emotionally - feeling ashamed or lossing interest in the things you love
  • physically - tired (loss of sleep), or experiencing symptoms like stomach aches and headaches

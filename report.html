How to Report Cyberbullying

If you are being cyberbullied or see it happening to someone else, report it right away to the platform or app where it's occurring. You can also tell a parent, teacher, or other trusted adult who can help you take action. Many platforms like Facebook, Twitter, and Instagram have built-in reporting mechanisms for such behaviors. Always keep evidence of the cyberbullying, such as screenshots, to support your report.

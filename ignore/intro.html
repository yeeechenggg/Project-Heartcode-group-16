Impersonation

  • Definition:
    • Pretending to be someone else with deceptive intent.
  • Intent:
    • Deceit, give misleading information, or gaining unauthorized access.

    Outing & Trickery

  • Definition:
    • Revealing something individuals may wish to keep private.
    • Eg. Sexual orientation, gender identity.
  • Intent:
    • Exposing personal details to mislead or manipulate others.

    Online Harrrassment

  • Definition:
    • Aggressive behavior directed towards individuals through digital platforms.
  • Intent:
    • Cause emotional distress.


    Exclusion

  • Definition:
    • Intentionally excluding individuals from online activities or social circles.
  • Intent:
    • Cause impact on the target's self-esteem and mental well-being.

    Cyberstalking

  • Definition:
    • Using the internet to repeatedly and obsessively monitor, or harass someone.
  • Intent:
    • To instill fear, intimidate, or control the target.

    Doxxing

  • Definition:
    • Revealing private information about an individual without consent.
    • Eg. Home address, Phone number, Email.
  • Intent:
    • To inflict harm, harassment, or intimidation.
